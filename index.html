About Us

Welcome to Nile Orthopedic and Rehabilitation Association, Inc.

At Nile Orthopedic, we provide the highest standers of Orthopedic surgery and Podiatry care. Our team of Orthopedic surgeons and podiatrist adopts the most advanced techniques and delivers individualized care for our patients.
We believe that healthcare should be delivered locally to our patients, to allow personal communication with their providers. We cater the best Orthopedic and Podiatry care to all our patients in San Bernardino County, Riverside County, the Inland Empire and all the neighboring communities through our different office locations in the cities of Loma Linda, Riverside, Rancho Cucamonga, and Beaumont.

Our Doctors

Dr. BATA

Ahmed Bata, MD is highly regarded orthopedic surgeon. He is the CEO of Nile Orthopedic and Rehabilitation Association, Inc. Prior to this, he severed our community as Associate Professor of Orthopedic surgery at Loma Linda University Medical Center. Dr. Bata had extensive orthopedic training internationally and in North America. That included fellowship training at Pennsylvania hospital University of Pennsylvania and Hospital for Special Surgery Cornell University. He is a member of the American Academy of Orthopedic surgeon (AAOS) and American Orthopedic Foot and Ankle Society (AOFAS). Dr. Bata provides general orthopedic care. Dr. Bata understands the impact of orthopedic conditions on patients and their families. He is committed to provide you with the care you deserve.

Dr. Perez

Hugo Perez, DPM did an extensive three-year surgical residence in Seattle that included training at world renowned Harborview Medical Center and a mini-AO Fellowship in Dresden Germany. Dr Perez has extensive experience in treating all aspects of foot and ankle conditions including heel pain, diabetic ulcers, sports injuries, ingrown nails bunions and hammer toes. He has worked for large organizations such as Kaiser Permanente and UC Davis Medical Group before coming to Nile Orthopedics and Rehabilitation Association. Dr. Perez has published multiple scholarly papers and research has been published in prominent textbooks. Having foot pain can be very frustrating and it can keep you from what you need to do. Dr Perez understands how important it is to get you relief from your pain so you can get back to being you.
