About Us

Welcome to Nile Orthopedic and Rehabilitation Association, Inc.
Ahmed Bata, MD, is a highly regarded orthopedic surgeon who is known for his advanced techniques and individualized care for patients. He is the CEO of Nile Orthopedic and Rehabilitation Association, Inc., which is located in three convenient areas in Loma Linda, Rancho Cucamonga, and Riverside, California.
The practice provides premium orthopedic care to all the neighboring communities of Loma Linda, Colton, Riverside, Redlands, Moreno Valley, San Bernardino, and the Inland Empire region.
